2. Information we receive

Information you choose to send

If you submit the contact form, we receive your name, email address, company, and the details of your inquiry. Formspree processes the submission on our behalf, stores it in the form dashboard, and forwards a notification to our designated inbox. See the Formspree Privacy Policy for information about its practices. Please do not send sensitive personal information unless it is necessary and we have agreed on a secure way to receive it.

Technical and hosting information

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit, GitHub may process technical information needed to deliver and protect the site, such as an IP address, browser and device details, requested URLs, and timestamps. See the GitHub General Privacy Statement for information about GitHub’s practices.

Cookies and analytics

Verdent Labs does not currently set cookies, use advertising trackers, or run website analytics on this site. If that changes, we will update this policy and, where required, request consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

3. How and why we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and take steps requested before entering into a contract.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the site based on our legitimate interests in providing a reliable online presence.
  • To establish, exercise, or defend legal claims and comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not use website visitor data for solely automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Sharing and international transfers

We may share personal information with service providers that help us operate the site or communicate with you, including GitHub Pages, Formspree, and email providers; with professional advisers where necessary; or when required by law. We do not sell personal information.

Providers may process information outside your country, including outside the European Economic Area. Where required, a real Verdent Labs entity must put an approved transfer mechanism and appropriate safeguards in place and describe them here.

5. How long we keep information

Contact-form submissions and email inquiries are kept only as long as reasonably necessary to respond and manage the resulting business relationship. As a draft default, routine inquiries should be deleted or anonymized from the Formspree dashboard and our inbox within 24 months after the last interaction unless a longer period is needed for a contract, legal obligation, dispute, or security matter. Service providers may apply their own documented retention periods to technical and backup records.

6. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live and the circumstances, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, restriction of, or portability of your personal information. You may also have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests and to withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.

To make a request, email privacy@verdentlabs.example. We may need to verify your identity. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the data protection authority in your country; EU and EEA authorities are listed by the European Data Protection Board.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No online service or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

This business website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our practices or legal obligations change. The effective date above will show when the latest version took effect.
